Edviron Financial & Operations Dashboard:

Overview:
The Edviron Financial & Operations Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of payment processing performance across various ERP partners. Key insights include Total Gross Merchandise Value (GMV), revenue splits, payment gateway success rates, and user payment preferences. This dashboard helps in identifying operational bottlenecks, understanding transaction trends, and evaluating partner-level risk, facilitating data-driven decision-making for financial operations.

Key Features :-
Financial Overview
High-Level Metrics: Total GMV, ERP Revenue, Total Transactions, and Edviron Gross Revenue.

Revenue Splits: Clear visualization of the revenue margin retained by Edviron versus payouts owed to ERP partners.

Time-Based Trends: Analysis of transaction volume and GMV velocity over specific date ranges.


Partner Distribution: Breakdown of transaction volume and overall financial contribution by each ERP partner (SparkIT, CampusPro, Yahweh, AKIRA).

Payment & Gateway Insights
Gateway Efficiency: GMV contribution tracked by specific payment gateways to monitor routing volume.

Method Preferences: User adoption rates for different payment methods, highlighting the heavy dominance of UPI.

User Engagement: Tracking the active user base and the total number of unique users interacting with the platform.

Visualizations
Top-Level KPI Cards: High-level summaries displaying Total GMV, ERP Revenue, Total Transactions, Success Rate %, Edviron Gross Revenue, and Unique Users.

Gateway Donut Chart: Illustrating the percentage of GMV Contribution split between payment gateways.

Partner Health Matrix: A detailed table utilizing conditional formatting to display Total GMV, Success Rate %, and Pending Exposure by ERP Partner.

Partner Distribution Table: Presenting raw transaction counts, total financial volume, and ERP-specific revenue.

Trend Line Chart: Depicting Total GMV and Transaction volume trends across daily, weekly, and monthly timelines.

Payment Method Treemap: Showcasing the volume distribution across UPI, Credit Cards, Wallets, and Net Banking in an easily digestible format.

Revenue Split Stacked Column Chart: Visually comparing Edviron Net Revenue versus ERP Revenue for each individual partner.
